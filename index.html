Snake Game

By Shivam Maurya

Score: 0

  • When window loads game starts.
  • For movement of the snake you can use the arrow keys and also the WASD keys.
  • When the player eats the food the size of snake will grow of one block.
  • When the snake hit the walls then he will die and the alert pops up.
  • When the snake eats himself then also he will die and the alert pops up.
  • After player death player require to reload the page for restarting the game.
